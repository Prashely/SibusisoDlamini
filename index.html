Meet Sibusiso

Upcoming Crypto Millionaire

Sibusiso strongly believes that life-changing money can be achieved through cryptocurrency. However, he also knows that patience, consistency, and investing in the right tokens are vital factors in making that happen. Over the past four years, he has dedicated countless hours to researching tokens and investing in those he believes will change his life. Most importantly, he has never wavered in his commitment to his investments, even during the hurdles of the cryptocurrency market. In 2021, Sibusiso was able to turn R1,500 into R15,000 through crypto. Since then, he has helped countless people get started, whether through his courses or speaking engagements at the University of Witwatersrand.
